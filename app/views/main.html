Company shuttle buses to take you to office

Morning Shuttle Bus Arrangement

Located along Tolo Harbour, the choices of public transit to Hong Kong Science Park are very limited. Several MTR and bus transfers need to be made in order to arrive to the venue. Furthermore, public transit time can exceed an hour for staff who lives far from the office.To reduce travelling time and eliminate excessive gas transmission, staff is encouraged to take morning shuttle buses to work. Shuttle bus routes cover Hong Kong East/West, Kowloon East/West, New Territory South/North and Kowloon Tong; therefore, staff can leverage the services to not only arrive to work on time but also make fresher and cleaner air.

Evening Shuttle Bus Arrangement

Shuttle buses depart from Science Park office at 6 PM sharp and take expressways to travel to various stops in Hong Kong Island, Kowloon and New Territories. Each route has two to three drop off points for which staff can get off at location that is closest to their next destination. If staff stays after normal office hour, minivan service is arranged for pick up at 6:30 PM and 7:30 PM respectively.
